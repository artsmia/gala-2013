Gala After Party

Friday, June 22

9 p.m. to 1 a.m.

The party not to be missed!

Complimentary valet parking

Light bites & cash bar

Exhibition preview of Rembrandt in America from 9 to 10 p.m

Complimentary signature cocktail

VIP access to all entertainment

Cocktail attire (with a hint of red)



Inspired by the city of Amsterdam, this one-of-a-kind event will enliven the senses. Enjoy great entertainment, experience the mystique of the MIA at night, mix and mingle with a sell-out crowd, and dance the night away. See the MIA transformed by the cosmopolitan spirit of this artistic city!
